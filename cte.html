Veterans Memorial High School

About Our School

Veterans Memorial High School promotes numerous student clubs and organizations. Students are encouraged to participate in any of the many extracurricular activities offered at our campus: fine arts, athletics, student council, and a host of other clubs and organizations. Additionally, our school promotes student involvement in all district co-curricular activities (including Science Fair, History Fair, and UIL Academics). Many of these activities lead to campus level competitions where the winners of these contests move on to compete at the District, Regional, State, and National levels.
